About us

Black Light Records is a small well assorted record joint. We are record collectors ourselves, and we take part in several record fairs in Denmark.

Black Light Records specializes in collectable alternative / indie rock, punk, electronica, lo-fi, industrial, experimental, avantgarde and exotica.

Your always welcome to take a visit in our store and we'll be happy to answer some of your questions, help you find the perfect record, or just for a chit chat.

- Hope to see you in our cozy little store at Fredensgade in Aarhus
